Tips and Tricks
===========================
You can add org.cyclonedx:cyclonedx-maven-plugin to customise your SBOM output

<plugin>
  <groupId>org.cyclonedx</groupId>
  <artifactId>cyclonedx-maven-plugin</artifactId>
  <version>X.X.X</version>
</plugin>
